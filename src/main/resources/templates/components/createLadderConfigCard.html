Manage Groups

Create a new group, or view your existing groups below.

Create a group

Start a group here. After you create it, you will get an invite QR code and a link you can copy and send to other people so they can join. Your group will not be listed publicly, so only people you share the invite with can join. You can disable the invitation link and QR code at any time, or leave a group you started to schedule it for deletion at a later date. You can also manage members, such as promoting them to admin, or banning them from the group.

Join a group

Groups are join-by-invite only. Ask a group admin to send you their invite link, or scan their QR code with your phone camera to open the same link. Groups are not listed publicly.

Personal activity tracking

You can also create a group just for yourself. On the next page, use Casual match confirmation and turn on personal record matches when all opponents are guests. That lets you log your own matches against guests to track stats and earn activity-based trophies.
