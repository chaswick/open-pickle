Welcome to PicklBuddies

Congratulations, you have set up your PicklBuddies account.

There is nothing left to do until you are ready to head to the courts and play.

When It Is Time To Play

Only one member of your group needs to create a session.

Everyone else can join that same session, then you can play your pickleball games.

One Player Logs The Result

After a match is finished, one player from that match should log the result in the session.

You do not need every player to enter the score. One entry is enough.

The Other Team Confirms It

Once the match is logged, one player from the opposing team confirms it from the match dashboard.

You can confirm matches right away or wait until the end of the session. Either way works.

That Is The Whole PicklBuddies Flow

Join a session or private league, play some pickleball, one player logs the result, and one player on the opposing team confirms it.

After that, check the standings and keep an eye on your stats and trophies under User Account.
