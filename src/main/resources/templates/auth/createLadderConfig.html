How sessions work

Sessions reuse the normal invite code, link, and QR join flow, but they do not create their own seasons or standings.

Players who join this session become the roster you can choose from on the match logger, and logged matches count toward the active competition season.

Tournament defaults

Tournament leagues are created with manual start/stop seasons, standard opponent confirmation, and an immediate season start.

Tournament mode also requires season matches to line up with an active round-robin matchup, and only league admins can start or manage round robins.
