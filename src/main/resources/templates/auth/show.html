Your Session Is Ready

You started this session, so you are the one who shares it with everyone else.

Use the Invite Buddies card for the shared code, QR, or nearby sharing when your group is at the courts.

Watch The Standings

The standings card doubles as your live roster, so you can see who is in and how the session is going at a glance.

Use the Players card only for admin tasks like join requests and removals.

Run Matches Or Start A Round Robin

The match buttons in the session header are where your group logs results, opens confirmations, and starts a round robin if you want structured pairings.

Once a round robin starts, this page becomes the hub for the active assignment and round tabs.

End The Session When Play Is Over

When you are done, use the leave button to end the session. As the starter, that closes it out for everyone.

Until then, keep the invite handy and use the confirmation popup whenever a result needs attention.

You Are In The Session

The session starter already handled setup, so your job is to play and use this page for the match flow.

You do not need to create another session for the same set of games.

One Player Logs The Match

After each game, one player from the match should use the match buttons in the session header to log the result.

Voice log or classic log both work. Only one person needs to enter the score.

The Other Team Confirms It

Once the result is logged, one player from the other team confirms it from the confirmation popup here or later from Confirm Matches.

You can confirm right away or wait until the end of the session if that is easier for your group.

Watch The Standings And Leave When Done

The standings card shows who is in the session, where you sit in the session results, and your global rank when it exists.

When you are finished playing, leave the session here. Your confirmed results still count toward the competition season.
