Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

This public repo ships a placeholder privacy-policy template. Deployment operators should replace the operator identity, contact information, and domain details before launching a public service.

1. Who We Are

Open-Pickle ("we," "us," or "our") is community software that may be operated by the operator of the deployment you are using. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including:

  • Account Information: Email address, username, encrypted password
  • Profile Information: Display name, skill level, and preferences
  • Activity Data: Match results, group participation, and comments
  • Location Data (Optional): Current device location and submitted location names if you use the Check In feature
  • Voice Feature Data (Optional): Speech-to-text transcripts (not raw audio) and related metadata if you use voice-assisted match logging
  • Communications: Messages you send to us

We also collect certain information automatically, such as:

  • IP address
  • Browser and device type
  • Pages visited and usage patterns
  • Cookies and similar technologies

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service
  • Display group standings and match results
  • Improve website functionality and user experience
  • Monitor security and prevent abuse
  • Send service-related communications
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Location-Based Check In

Check In is optional. If you use it, Open-Pickle requests your current location only after you tap the Check In button and approve the browser or installed progressive web app (PWA) permission prompt. We use that location to identify or create a nearby play location, associate your account with an active check-in, support saved location names, and show active player counts within the feature. We do not use Check In for background or continuous location tracking.

5. Public Information

Because Open-Pickle is a community platform, certain information such as display names, rankings, match results, and public comments are visible to other users. Please avoid sharing sensitive personal information in public areas.

6. Community Funding and Donations

If you choose to support the Service through a voluntary donation or similar community funding channel, payment processing will be handled by third-party providers. We do not store full credit card numbers on our servers.

7. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services such as hosting providers, analytics tools, speech recognition providers (if voice features are enabled), and payment processors (if applicable). These providers may process limited technical information as necessary to provide their services.

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to maintain login sessions, remember preferences, analyze usage patterns (if analytics is enabled), and improve performance. You can manage cookies through your browser settings.

9. Data Retention

We retain account information while your account is active. Historical match results may be retained for standings integrity and community continuity. We may retain certain information as required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

10. Data Security

We implement reasonable safeguards including encrypted transmission (HTTPS), secure password hashing, limited administrative access, and periodic security review. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is completely secure.

11. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at support@example.invalid.

12. U.S. State Privacy Notice

We do not sell personal information. We do not use sensitive personal information for behavioral advertising. We collect and use personal information only as described in this Policy.

13. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under 13. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

15. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Contact us at support@example.invalid.
