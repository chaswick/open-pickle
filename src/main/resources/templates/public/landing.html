Why use it

This is not trying to replace Elo or DUPR.

Those systems are built to estimate long-term player skill. This is built to measure something different: who is performing best over a defined stretch of time against the people they actually play.

One season, two ways to compete

Track your place on the global leaderboard and your standing against the players you actually play. So even if the overall top spot is far away, your season still has real stakes.

Season trophies and ratings bands

Earn trophies at the end of the season based on how you did against the players you actually competed with, as well as over all.

Measures performance

Built around real play patterns, it tells you how well you are playing right now, not from a cherry-picked set of matches.
